Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a YouTube url to create a bundle?

No. In order to process your audio or video, our systems have to download it. Converting a YouTube url into a media url is challenging because they occasionally change how it's generated. We recommend you to use your raw media url directly.

What languages do you support?

At the time of this writing, English, Spanish, and French. We're always working to add more languages so if your language isn't listed, contact us as we might have your language in a private beta.

Where are you based?

Just east of downtown Austin, TX. If you're in town, you should drop by our offices. Just drop us a note to let us know when you want to visit.
