--- layout: quickstart title: Quickstart Guides --- These quickstarts are designed to get you started with the Clarify API quickly. With the exception of Human Transcripts, any of these quickstarts should take you 5-10 minutes to fully implement.

Media Search

The Media Search Quickstart demonstates how to get started with any media. The quickstart walks you through loading your first audio or video, searching for a few keywords, and exploring the results.

The Multi-Language Quickstart demonstrates get started with multiple languages. The quickstart walks you through loading your French media, searching for a French terms, and exploring the results.

The Keyword Insight Quickstart demonstrates how to extract the most frequently said words from any audio or video. The quickstart walks you through loading your audio/video, requesting keywords, and retrieving the results.

Topics Insight

See the Code »
The Topics Insight Quickstart demonstrates how to quickly extract the topics from any audio or video. The quickstart walk you through loading your audio/video, requesting topics, and retrieving the results.

The Human Transcript Quickstart demonstrates how to take any English audio and get a transcript. The quickstart walks you through loading your audio, requesting the transcript, and retrieving the results.
