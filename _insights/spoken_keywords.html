--- layout: quickstart title: Spoken Keywords navtitle: Spoken Keywords languages: - curl order: 3 --- The Spoken Keywords insight contains a list of:

keywords

Keywords are important terms spoken in the audio. The keywords list contains objects with: - term - the word - weight - a score which signifies the term's relevance - count - the number of times which the term was spoken

entities (invite only)

Named entities are proper nouns or other entities spoken in the audio. These include people's names, place names, organization names, dates, currency amounts etc. The entities list contains objects with: - term - the entity - weight - a score which signifies the term's relevance - type - the type of entity The possible type values are: - person - a person's name. This may be full name or just first or last name - location - a geographical location such as country, state, city, etc. - product - a product or brand name - organization - any type of organization or group - date - a full date or partial date such as a month, a year, a day of the week etc. - money - a monetary value spoken in the audio, for example "100 dollars" - percent - a percentage value spoken in the audio
